Detailed Description

This class accepts albaVMEVolumeGray as input and take a surface as additional input, then change the scalar density of the volumes' voxels inner the surface with a density value given by the user.

This operation is used in general after that a albaVMEVolumeGray is generated by the albaOpCreateVolume operation.

See also
albaOpCreateVolume, albaVMEVolumeGray
