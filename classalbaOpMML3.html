Detailed Description

albaOpMML3.


Muscle Modelling Lab.
Manual registration operation (register from template).
This operation registers a polydata model of a muscle or bone onto a volume image of the feature.
The inputs are a volume (the patient data) and a muscle surface (the model).
Global registration is performed by matching a set of 3 or 4 landmarks on patient and model.
The user can then manually fit the shape of the model to the patient data.
Optional inputs are landmarks used for registration and defining the slicing axis.
Two optional axis landmarks can be used to set the slicing direction.
The input landmarks must be in VME folders labelled "ATLAS" and "Patient",
with the axis landmarks being part of the Atlas section.
