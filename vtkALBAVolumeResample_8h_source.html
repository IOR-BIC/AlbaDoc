16// .NAME vtkALBAVolumeResample- a filter for creating slices from structured volume data (rectilinear grid or structured points)
17//
18// .SECTION Description
19// Inputs of the filter are:
20// volume (vtkRectilinearGrid or vtkImageData)
21// plane definition: origin and two vectors that define the axes of the image rectangle
22// color mapping: window and level of color mapping
23//
24// Outputs of the filter are:
25// image (vtkImageData): The resolution and spacing of the output volume image should be specified by consumer.
26// The origin will be automatically set to zero.
27// polyline slice (vtkPolyData): This output includes both the polyline that define the cut of a plane and corresponded triangles. The texture coordinates are provided as well.
28// .SECTION See Also
29// vtkProbeFilter, vtkPlaneSource
30
