13 vtkALBAVolumeTextureMapper2D is equivalent to the standard vtkVolumeTextureMapper2D
14 but it does not suffer from incorrect rendering when the view renders also
15 polydata (e.g., gizmo) and also it does not rebuild texture every time, only if
16 it is necessary (e.g., resolution has changed, camera moved)
17*/
