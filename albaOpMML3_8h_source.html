73//------------------------------------------------------------------------------
85//
86// Coordinate systems.
87// Atlas space - the coord system of the model before global registration.
88// Patient space - the coord system of the patient data. The 3d view is in patient space.
89// Current slice space - the coord system of the current slice. The 2d view is in current slice space.
90// Alpha and zeta - alpha and zeta are the distance (fractional and actual) of the slice along the axis.
91// The splines are functions of zeta, but displayed as a function of slice id.
92//
93// Transforms
94// vtkPlane/vtkCutter requires an inverse transform.
95//
96//------------------------------------------------------------------------------
