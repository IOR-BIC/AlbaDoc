13 vtkALBAVolumeRayCastMapper is equivalent to the standard vtkVolumeRayCastMapper
14 but it does not suffer from incorrect rendering when the view renders also
15 polydata (e.g., gizmo) and also it does not recast rays every time, only if
16 it is necessary (e.g., resolution has changed, camera moved)
17*/
